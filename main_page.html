Get to Know Yumi Jason Sinn!

Yuni Sinn, an accomplished violinist with a passion for both classical and contemporary music, has captivated audiences with her stunning performances and expressive playing style. A graduate of prestigious institutions, Yuni honed her craft under the guidance of renowned mentors, mastering the intricate techniques required for a diverse range of musical genres. Her talents have led her to perform with major orchestras across the country, where her skill and charisma shine through every note. Beyond her skills as a performer, Yuni is also deeply committed to music education, inspiring the next generation of musicians through workshops and community outreach programs. She believes in the transformative power of music and works tirelessly to share that love with others.

Yuni's dedication to her craft, combined with her infectious enthusiasm, makes her a cherished member of the Colorado Symphony, and her solo performances never fail to leave a lasting impression on audiences. As she prepares for upcoming concerts, including special presentations like "The Nightmare Before Christmas," fans eagerly anticipate the magic she will bring to the stage, blending technical mastery with heartfelt emotion to create unforgettable musical moments.
